Corporate profile page

Shangri-La Group is a multi-asset, global corporation with deep and extensive expertise in creating experiences and iconic landmarks that showcase the best of Asia and beyond.

Shangri-La Asia Limited is our investment holding arm and the activities of the Company’s subsidiaries own and manage hotels and mixed-use developments.

Incorporated in Bermuda with limited liability. Shangri-La Asia Limited is listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (stock code 00069) with a secondary listing on the Singapore Exchange Securities Trading Limited (stock code Shang Asia 2kHK$)

CSR & Governance

Shangri-La Hotels & Resorts is recognised by the Dow Jones Sustainability Indices (DJSI) as the only hotel group included in the index component within Asia/Pacific. It affirms our commitment to sustainable operations and our leadership within the global travel & tourism sector.

Overseas Listed Subsidiaries

Our strong presence in regional Asian markets.

Headquartered in Asia, we have local intel and in-house expertise in the region’s fastest growing markets. Our listed subsidiaries include Shangri-La Hotels Berhad (Malaysia) and Shangri-La Hotel Public Company Limited (Thailand).

Malaysia
Our subsidiary Shangri-La Hotels (Malaysia) Berhad owns and operates hotels and beach resorts, a golf course, investment properties and commercial laundry. Shangri-La Hotels (Malaysia) Berhad is incorporated with limited liability in Malaysia and traded on the Bursa Malaysia Securities Berhad.

Thailand
Our subsidiary Shangri-La Hotel Public Company Limited (Thailand) is an investment holding company whose principal activities are investment properties and operation of hotels and resorts. Shangri-La Hotel Public Company Limited is incorporated with limited liability in Thailand and traded on the Stock Exchange of Thailand.
