Our investment holdings

Our subsidiary Shangri-La Hotel Public Company Limited (Thailand) is an investment holding company whose principal activities are investment properties and operation of hotels and resorts.


Shangri-La Hotel Public Company Limited is incorporated with limited liability in Thailand and traded on the Stock Exchange of Thailand (stock code SHANG).
