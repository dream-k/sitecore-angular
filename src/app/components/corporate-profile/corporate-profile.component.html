Our investment holdings

Shangri-La Group is a multi-asset, global corporation with deep and extensive expertise in creating experiences and iconic landmarks that showcase the best of Asia and beyond.


Shangri-La Asia Limited is our investment holding company and the principal activities of Shangri-La Group are development, ownership and operations and management of hotel properties, investment properties as well as commercial and residential developments for sale.


At Shangri-La Group, we operate our businesses under our multi-brands encompassing key areas in hotels and resorts, commercial and residential properties, food and beverage and recreational experiences; including but not limited to “Shangri-La”, “Kerry Hotel”, “Hotel Jen”, “Traders Hotel”, “Rasa”, “Summer Palace”, “Shang Palace”, “CHI, The Spa at Shangri-La”.


Incorporated in Bermuda with limited liability. Shangri-La Asia Limited is listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (stock code 00069) with a secondary listing on the Singapore Exchange Securities Trading Limited (stock code S07)
